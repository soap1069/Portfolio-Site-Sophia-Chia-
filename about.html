About

My Story

Hi! I'm Sophia, a UX designer who aspires to compassionate and ethical design that empowers people to achieve their goals without exploiting their attention or privacy.

Through my years working as a marketer in the travel tech industry, I've gained extensive experience around the creation and adoption of digital platforms. I had the pleasure to drive international product launch exhibitions and work with creatives to create new brand experiences.

I've come to realize that, though I am a generalist who craves excitement through variety, the times when I feel most happy are those when I am designing. Whether its conceptualizing the design of a newsfeed based on testing insights, or designing a highly converting customer purchase flow from ad to check out, I love the process of finding the right way to present information and interactions that would bring the most impact.

I am a storyteller, experience weaver, and a fool who wants to learn everything to create the things that I'm passionate about. And my greatest assets are my empathy, work ethic, and insatiable appetite for learning.

I constantly seek collaboration with people or companies that share a vision.

Some things I truly love:

  • Walks in nature
  • Connecting over conversation
  • Singing my heart out on the ukelele
  • Standup comedy
  • The School of Life

Originally from Taiwan, I now live in Berlin, Germany. If you would like to exchange or collaborate, feel free to drop a line.
